Flash Card Manager

While starting out learning Japanese, I found that memorising hundreds of Kanji characters was going to be challenging.

I decided to create an application to create and store and test myself on flash cards. At the time I was using Unity and just decided to go with what I knew.

It took a while to get the logic working, but we got there in the end, and it's now available for anyone to download on itch.io
